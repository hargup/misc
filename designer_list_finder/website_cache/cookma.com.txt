Our practice focuses on customized, strategic transactions, developed to serve the needs of private equity investors, family offices, corporations, executives and business owners. We help navigate the details and complexities necessary to arrive at lasting, quality acquisitions.
WHO WE ARECook M&A is recognized as one of the leading buy-side M&A advisory firms in the industry. Our M&A advisory team delivers transaction focused strategic advice to private equity investors, family offices, corporations, executives and owners in buy-side transactions. We work with businesses across all industry sectors and sizes to understand the market, identify potential acquisition targets, assist in due diligence and resolve transaction issues throughout the process. HOW WE WORKAs your exclusive partner, we prepare macroeconomic industry research and make recommendations tailored to your industry that are specific to your goals. None of our target lists is ever the same. We follow established research-driven processes that deliver streamlined solutions from the initial research phase all the way through to a signed LOI.Read More
WHY WE'RE DIFFERENTWhile no two deals are the same, the level of discipline we bring never varies.
