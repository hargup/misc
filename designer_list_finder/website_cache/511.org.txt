 BART Schedule Improvements in Effect
BART has implemented an updated service plan that is responsive to post-pandemic commute patterns and ridership growth opportunities. For more info visit the BART website.




 

 Take Bay Trail SFO Gap Study Survey!
The Bay Trail SFO Gap Study will identify potential Bay Trail alignments to close a three-mile Bay Trail gap around SFO. To participate in the survey, visit the MTC website.




 

 Express Carpool Check Pilot Program
MTC is seeking volunteers to test a carpool verification app on a portion of the the I-680 Contra Costa Express Lanes. For more information, visit the MTC website.




 

 Start Saving with Express Lanes START℠
Low-income drivers can save 50-75% on I-880 Express Lanes tolls. Apply here.




 

 MTC's Richmond-San Rafael Bridge E-Bike Commute Program 
Want to see if you're eligible for up to $1,000 for an e-bike? Visit rsrebikecommute.org
