Wiki News and Updates
Manga - The Dragon Ball Super manga, authored by Toyotarō and Akira Toriyama, continues to release new chapters generally on the 20th of each month on the official Viz website. The new Super Hero Saga began in December 2022. The Ultra God Mission!!!! manga will conclude in October 2023.
Anime - Super Dragon Ball Heroes, a promotional anime based on the video game Dragon Ball Heroes finished airing its Supreme Kai of Time Saga in August 2023. The Demon Invader Saga will begin later in the year.
Video Games - In March 2023, a new Budokai Tenkaichi video game was announced. Dragon Ball Z: Kakarot has received Season 2 DLC content in 2023 based on the Bardock - The Father of Goku TV special and the Piccolo Jr. Saga.
Articles - In June 2023 we reached 8,100 articles. Next goal is 8,500. Great job everyone! 
Videos - We now encourage users to upload premium, licensed videos, which can be found in the Wikia Video Library. Other videos are not allowed due to copyright issues. Please experiment with the video upload and embedding tools, but do not add them to articles at this time.
Guidelines - Remember that all users must familiarize themselves with the Rules. It is important that these guidelines be followed across all areas of the site such as on articles, blogs, and of course the chat feature as well.

Featured Article
The Survivor, Granolah

Granolah is a bounty hunter and the sole survivor of the Cerealian race after it was annihilated by the Saiyans under the Frieza Force. After hearing of Frieza's revival, his desire for revenge is reinvigorated.
He is the initial the main antagonist of the Granolah the Survivor Saga, the most recent story arc of the Dragon Ball Super manga series.
